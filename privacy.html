Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TeAndrea Burnett Tutoring (“Company,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects personal information provided by parents or legal guardians.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you schedule or communicate with us:

  • Parent/guardian name
  • Student name and grade level
  • Contact information (phone number and/or email)
  • Scheduling details
  • Relevant academic or learning information voluntarily provided

2. How Information Is Used

Information collected is used solely for:

  • Scheduling and session coordination
  • Communication with parents/guardians
  • Instructional planning and academic support
  • Administrative record keeping

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or distribute personal information to third parties. Information may only be disclosed if required by law or necessary to protect the safety of a student.

4. Data Storage & Security

Reasonable administrative and technical safeguards are used to protect personal information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is guaranteed to be 100% secure.

5. Children’s Privacy

All information is submitted by a parent or legal guardian. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children.

6. Media & Consent

Student images, recordings, or identifying information will not be published without written parental consent.

7. Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as necessary for instructional and administrative purposes, or as required by applicable law.

8. Your Rights

Parents/guardians may request to review, correct, or delete their submitted information by contacting the Company directly.

9. Policy Updates

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued participation in tutoring services constitutes acceptance of any updates.

10. Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact TeAndrea Burnett Tutoring through the website’s contact page.
