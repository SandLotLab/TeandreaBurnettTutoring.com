About TeAndrea Burnett

I’m a classroom teacher in Troy, Alabama and I created this tutoring service to give families high-quality support outside regular school hours.

My tutoring style is patient, clear, and practical. I focus on core reading and math habits that students can carry back into the classroom with confidence.

  • Consistent routines that help children feel secure and successful
