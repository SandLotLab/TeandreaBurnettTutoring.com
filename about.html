Meet TeAndrea Burnett

Local tutoring support for Troy, Alabama families who want strong foundations and confident learners.

I started tutoring because I believe early learning years shape how children see themselves. When a child feels successful in reading or math, they walk into school with more confidence, ask better questions, and stay open to learning.

My sessions are built for kindergarten through 3rd grade students who need clear instruction, patient support, and consistent practice. I work closely with families in Troy, AL to focus on the exact skills each child needs most, from phonics and reading fluency to math facts and word problems.

I care deeply about creating a safe, encouraging space where children can make mistakes, try again, and keep growing. Parents in Troy, Alabama deserve tutoring they can trust, and students deserve support that helps them feel capable every step of the way.

My teaching philosophy

I teach in a way that is direct, calm, and encouraging. I break lessons into manageable steps, celebrate small wins, and keep expectations high without making children feel overwhelmed.

What makes these sessions different

  • Small groups with up to 5 students
  • Focused instruction matched to your child's needs
  • Safe and encouraging learning environment
  • Steady support that builds confidence over time

Subjects covered

  • Reading Fluency
  • Phonics
  • Comprehension & Writing
  • Math Fluency
  • Word Problems
  • Science & Social Studies Support
