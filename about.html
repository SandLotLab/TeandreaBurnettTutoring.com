About TeAndrea

I’m a 1st grade teacher in Troy, Alabama. I built this tutoring service for families who want steady, practical support outside the school day.

My approach is simple: teach clearly, practice intentionally, and keep parents informed. I focus first on reading and math fluency because those skills unlock progress in every other subject.

What families can expect

  • Teacher-led instruction with classroom-tested strategies
  • A calm and encouraging learning environment
  • Clear session focus and age-appropriate expectations
  • Respectful handling of student accommodation needs
