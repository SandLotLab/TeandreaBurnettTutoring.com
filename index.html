FAQ

How do I know if my child needs tutoring?

If reading feels slow, math facts are hard to recall, homework causes frustration, or confidence is slipping, tutoring can help build the foundation before gaps grow.

Do you tutor one-on-one or small group?

Sessions may include small groups (up to 5 students). Instruction stays structured and age-appropriate.

What should my child bring?

A folder, pencil, and any recent schoolwork or teacher notes you want me to review.

Can you support reading and math in the same session?

Yes. We prioritize what matters most and structure the hour for steady practice and progress.
