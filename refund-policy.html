Refund Policy

Session fee is $25 per hour.

If you cancel at least 24 hours before the appointment, you can choose a full refund or reschedule credit.

Cancellations within 24 hours are normally non-refundable, but a one-time reschedule may be offered when space allows.

No-show appointments are non-refundable. If the tutor must cancel, families can choose a full refund or priority reschedule.
